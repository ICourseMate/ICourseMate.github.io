Welcome to Coursemate!

Coursemate is an extension designed to help NTPU students store and manage their class schedules with ease. Say goodbye to the hassle of manual planning and organization, and let Coursemate do the work for you.

With Coursemate, you can:

  • Import your class schedule from NTPU
  • Set reminders for class times
  • Easily import it to your google calendar
